MARANHÃO

Maranhão is one of the 27 federative units in Brazil, located in the Northeast region encompassing the Mid-Northern subregion of the country. The state borders three Brazilian states: Piauí (east), Tocantins (south and southwest) and Pará (west), in addition to the Atlantic Ocean (north). With an area of 331,937,450 km² and with 217 municipalities, it is the second largest state in the Northeast region and the eighth largest state in Brazil. With a population of 7,153,262, is the 11th most populous state in the country.The capital and most populous city is São Luís.more info
